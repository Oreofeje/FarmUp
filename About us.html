Who We Are

Farm Fresh's journey commenced out of the desire to see Africans live a good healthy life in a world where unhealthy activities leading to terminal illnesses are daily reported to be claiming the lives of many. Overpopulation has forced food scarcity into the heart of both big and developing cities and town and to combact, the government and entreprenuers of nations of the world have resulted to using chemical to enhance food production and preservation.

We being sisters, wives and mothers to our loved ones have individually practiced Organic Farming and have formed an alliance which has resulted in the creation of our company Farm Fresh.
